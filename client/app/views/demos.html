Demos
See how you can use and analyze our data to build compelling visualizations.

Globe
A globe made with three.js that plots a subset of Tweets based on geolocation data. Not all Tweets related to a keyword will contain latitude and longitude information.

Wordcloud
A wordcloud made with D3.js that analyzes a set of Tweets that contain the keyword "pizza." The larger the word in the wordcloud, the more frequently the word appears in Tweets; the more blue the word is, the more positive the sentiment. Redder words have a more negative sentiment value.
